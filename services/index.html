Service questions

Before you book.

A few practical details to help you book the right clean with confidence.

Ask our team

Standard Clean maintains an already-kept home and focuses on the main everyday surfaces. Deep Clean allows more time for detail work, built-up dust and areas that need attention beyond regular upkeep.

Most extras can be combined with most services, although availability may depend on the service, property condition and time booked. The calculator shows the relevant choices for your selection.

You choose the total time and provide a priority list. The cleaner works through that list in order, but completion of every task cannot be guaranteed within a fixed number of hours.

Recurring discounts apply to Standard and Deep cleans: 10% for weekly or fortnightly bookings and 5% for bookings every three or four weeks.

Bond decisions remain with the landlord or property manager. We focus on a thorough vacate checklist and offer re-clean support for eligible agreed items reported within seven days.

Cleaners can bring professional products. Where possible, customers provide a working vacuum and mop suited to their floors. Tell us in advance if this is not possible so we can organise accordingly.
