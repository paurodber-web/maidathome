Common questions

A few useful details before booking.

Full service inclusions and exclusions are available on each individual service page.

What is the difference between Standard and Deep cleaning?
Standard Clean focuses on maintaining the main areas and everyday surfaces of a generally cared-for home. Deep Clean allows more time and attention for built-up dust and detailed work.
Do I need to provide cleaning products?
Cleaning products are included. To help avoid cross-contamination between homes, clients generally provide a mop and vacuum. If this is not possible, let us know so we can try to assign a suitably equipped team.
Can I add extras to any service?
Most extras can be added to flat-rate and hourly bookings. Some are already included in End of Lease cleaning, and steam cleaning is subject to specialist availability.
How do recurring discounts work?
Weekly and fortnightly clients receive 10% off, while three-weekly and four-weekly clients receive 5% off. The discount begins from the second completed service.
How does an Hourly Clean work?
You book a minimum of two hours and provide a priority list. The cleaner works through that list in order, but the service guarantees quality rather than a fixed number of completed areas.
