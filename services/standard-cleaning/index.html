What Is It

Maintenance cleaning, done to a
professional standard

A standard clean is designed to keep your home in consistently excellent condition. It's not a once-a-year event — it's the backbone of a well-maintained home, delivered on whatever schedule suits you.

Our cleaners work through every room systematically using a structured checklist. That means nothing gets forgotten, nothing gets rushed, and every visit delivers the same quality — whether it's your first clean or your fifth.

We bring everything needed: commercial-grade equipment, eco-friendly and non-toxic products, and a cleaner who has been background-checked and trained to our standard. You don't need to supply a thing.

When to Book

Is a standard clean
right for you?

A standard clean is the right choice for most Melbourne households — but it's not the right tool for every situation. Here's when it fits perfectly.

🔄

You want a regular, consistent clean

Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly — a standard clean on a schedule is the most effective way to keep your home in great condition without thinking about it.

🏡

Your home is already reasonably tidy

Standard cleaning works best in homes that are maintained between visits. If there's no major buildup, a standard clean is efficient and thorough.

⏰

You're time-poor and want it handled

You'd rather spend your weekend on other things. Book once and your home stays consistently clean without any ongoing effort from you.

💰

You want predictable, transparent pricing

Flat rate by bedrooms. Know exactly what you'll pay before we arrive. No surprises, no variables — especially when booked on a recurring plan.

When standard cleaning might not be enough

If your home has particular needs, a different service may serve you better. Here's how to tell:

The home hasn't been professionally cleaned in over 2–3 months → consider a Deep Clean first
You're moving out and need to recover your bond → you need an End of Lease Clean
You're moving into a new property → a Move In Clean is the right starting point
You need the oven, fridge, or windows done → add these as Extras to any booking
