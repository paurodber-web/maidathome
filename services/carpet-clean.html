What is included

The carpet treatment process, step by step.

Treatment depends on fibre type, condition and safe access. Stain removal cannot be guaranteed.

Assess

Condition, fibres and visible concerns are reviewed first.

  • Review agreed rooms and access
  • Note visible stains and traffic lanes
  • Identify fibre or colour concerns
  • Discuss furniture and fragile items
  • Set realistic treatment expectations

Prepare

The treatment area is made safe and accessible.

  • Remove small floor items
  • Keep children and pets away
  • Provide safe power and access
  • Vacuum loose debris where appropriate
  • Protect nearby delicate belongings

Treat

Suitable products are applied to the agreed carpet.

  • Apply suitable pre-treatment
  • Focus on traffic areas
  • Attend compatible spots and spills
  • Work within fibre limitations
  • Avoid unsafe or damaged areas

Extract

Professional equipment lifts moisture and loosened soil.

  • Steam clean agreed rooms
  • Extract moisture and suspended soil
  • Treat accessible carpet edges
  • Check visible areas after extraction
  • Explain any remaining limitations

Dry

Aftercare protects the result while fibres dry.

  • Allow approximately 6–12 hours
  • Ventilate when weather allows
  • Avoid normal foot traffic
  • Keep pets and children away
  • Replace furniture only when fully dry

Deep fibre hygiene

Why professional steam cleaning matters.

Steam cleaning reaches below the surface to lift embedded soil and refresh carpet fibres. It can also reduce some trapped particles and odours, depending on the carpet and their cause.
