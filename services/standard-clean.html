Before the cleaner arrives

A little preparation protects the cleaning time.

Clear notes, safe access and the right equipment help the team focus on the work you booked.

01

Provide a mop and vacuum

Clients generally provide household equipment to reduce cross-contamination. Request an equipped team if this is not possible; availability may be limited.

02

Arrange reliable access

Use a lockbox, concierge or secure key location. Cleaners do not keep client keys between services.

03

Tell us about pets

Pets are welcome, but note allergies or fears and make sure they do not interfere with the cleaner.

04

Provide suitable parking

Share parking details before arrival so the cleaner can reach the property without losing booked time.

Outside the Standard scope

Some work needs another service or specialist.

Standard Clean is routine residential care. It does not cover hazardous work, heavy restoration, exterior maintenance or tasks requiring specialist equipment.

Standard Clean FAQs

Useful details before you book.

For wider questions about payments, changes and access, visit the complete FAQ page.

Can Standard Clean be booked as a one-off?
Yes. Choose a one-off service when the home needs a routine reset without committing to a regular schedule.
Can I request the same cleaner regularly?
We aim to keep the same cleaner for recurring clients whenever possible. Changes may occasionally be needed because of availability, illness or personal circumstances.
Does the cleaner bring products?
Yes. Standard cleaning products are included. Clients generally provide a mop and vacuum to reduce cross-contamination between homes.
How is Standard Clean priced?
Standard Clean uses a fixed rate based on bedrooms, bathrooms and the selected extras. It is not charged according to how many hours the cleaner spends in the home.
Can I add oven or window cleaning?
Yes. Select optional extras during booking. Their price is added clearly before you confirm the service.
