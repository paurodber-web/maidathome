Our Services

A standard clean covers essential maintenance: dusting accessible surfaces, vacuuming and mopping floors, cleaning toilet, shower/bath, kitchen countertops, and taking out bins. A deep clean goes much further, covering grout scrubbing, baseboards (skirting boards), dusting light fixtures, wiping door frames, cleaning behind appliances, and detailing tracks. We recommend a deep clean first if your home hasn't been professionally cleaned in over 3 months.
Our End of Lease clean is a specialized bond-back service aligned with rental checklist standards in Victoria. It includes cleaning inside all kitchen cupboards, deep oven and rangehood cleaning, bathroom descaling, window frames and glass (interior), skirtings, doors, tracks, and spot-cleaning walls. Carpet steam cleaning can be added as an extra. We offer a 72-hour bond-back guarantee.
Yes! We have dedicated balcony and window cleaning pages and services. You can add window interior/exterior cleaning, track vacuuming, balcony floor sweeping/mopping, and railing wipes to any regular clean or book them as standalone packages.

Booking & Scheduling

Not at all. Over 80% of our regular clients are out or at work during the clean. You can coordinate access via a secure lockbox code, key drop-off, or key release with your building's concierge. We will notify you via SMS when we arrive and once the service is complete.
It depends on the home size and service. A standard clean for a 1-2 bedroom home typically takes 2 to 3.5 hours. Larger 3-4 bedroom properties take 3.5 to 5 hours. Deep cleans and End of Lease cleans require extra time and are completed by teams of 1 or 2 professionals.
We understand schedules change. You can cancel or reschedule any booking completely free of charge up to 24 hours before the service. Cancellations within 24 hours of the booking may incur a late cancellation fee to cover the cleaner's scheduled time.
