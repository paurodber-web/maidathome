Hourly Rate

Pay for the time your home needs

Our hourly rate is the most flexible way to book. You're never locked into a scope — if a room needs extra attention, we spend the time. You pay only for what's worked.

When does hourly make sense?

Homes that haven't had a professional clean in a while, deep cleans, post-renovation cleanups, move-out situations, or any time the scope is uncertain — hourly ensures nothing gets rushed or skipped.

⏱

2-hour minimum applies to all hourly bookings. Most standard homes take 2.5–4 hours depending on size and condition. We'll give you an honest time estimate when you book.

FAQ

Pricing questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Still unsure? Call us on (03) 9999 0000 or email hello@maidathome.com.au — we'll recommend the right option for your home.

It depends on your home. For a well-maintained home on a regular schedule, flat rate is often more cost-effective because the time is predictable. For a home that hasn't been cleaned in a while or needs detailed attention, hourly is fairer — you're only charged for actual time worked, and nothing gets rushed to fit a fixed price.
Flat rates are based on the number of bedrooms in your home — nothing else. No bathroom surcharges, no size variables. A studio or 1-bedroom starts at $149, scaling up to $299 for 4+ bedrooms. Add-ons (oven, fridge, windows, etc.) are priced separately and added to the flat rate total.
Yes — you can choose your preferred pricing model at each booking. There's no lock-in. Some customers start with hourly for a first deep clean, then switch to flat rate for ongoing maintenance once the home is in a good baseline condition.
No. The price you see at booking is the price you pay. All equipment, eco-friendly products, and travel within the Melbourne metro area are included. The only additions are any extras or add-ons you specifically request, which are shown as separate line items.
Discounts are applied automatically at checkout when you select a recurring frequency — weekly (10% off), fortnightly (10% off), 3-weekly (5% off), or monthly (5% off). No codes needed. The discount applies to the base rate (flat or hourly) on every visit for as long as you maintain the schedule. You can pause or cancel any time with no penalty.
There's a 2-hour minimum for all hourly bookings. Most homes take between 2.5 and 4 hours depending on size and condition. We'll give you an honest time estimate when you book — and if the job finishes early, you're only charged for the actual time worked.
For hourly bookings, if the job looks like it'll run over the estimated time, your cleaner will contact you before continuing. Nothing extra is charged without your approval. For flat rate bookings, the price is fixed — no additional charges regardless of how long the clean takes.
