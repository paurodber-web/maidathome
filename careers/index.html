Who we look for

Cleaner
Requirements

We maintain a high rating in Melbourne by hiring candidates who exhibit attention to detail, reliability, and professionalism.

To join our cleaning crew, you must satisfy:

Prior residential or commercial cleaning experience (1-2 years preferred).
A current, clean Police Check (or willingness to obtain one).
Valid work rights in Australia (Citizen, PR, or Visa with work hours).
Reliable personal transport & Australian driver's license.
Good communication skills and fluency in English.
High-grade personal cleaning equipment & supplies (or we can assist).
