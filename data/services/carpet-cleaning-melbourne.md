## Deep Fiber Steam Cleaning Service

A quick vacuum can improve the look of a room without touching the dirt, dust and
residue buried deep in the carpet fibres. That is why we use hot water extraction to
flush out hidden grime that contributes to odours, dullness and that heavy feeling
carpets get after months of traffic.


Choosing a proper carpet steam clean over a superficial scrub means a deeper refresh,
not just a temporary improvement in appearance. It is especially useful in bedrooms,
living rooms, rental properties and homes with pets or young children.


## Stain & Odor Removal Specialist

Carpet problems are rarely just visual. A single spill can leave a stain, but traffic

## Carpet Cleaning FAQs

Find everything you need to know about our professional carpet steam cleaning service in Melbourne.


How long does Carpet Cleaning take?


For a typical Melbourne home, carpet cleaning takes between 1 and 3 hours. A
standard 3-bedroom house usually takes about 2 to 3 hours, depending on the condition of the
carpets and any specific stain treatments required.


How long does it take to dry?


Carpet steam cleaning typically requires 6 to 12 hours of drying time. In ideal
Melbourne weather with open windows and good airflow, dry times can be as low as 4 to 8
hours. We recommend waiting until the carpet is fully dry before resuming heavy use.


Do you remove stains completely?


While we have a very high success rate with professional-grade stain removers, some
permanent dyes (like red wine or old coffee) might not be 100% removable. However, we always
achieve the best possible result for your specific carpet type.


Is Carpet Cleaning safe for my family?


Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic products that are 100% safe for children and pets.
Our high-temperature steam also sanitizes your floors by killing 99.9% of bacteria without
leaving any harmful residues.
