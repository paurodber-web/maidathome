## Dish Washing FAQs

Answers about inclusions, preparation, recurring service and how dish washing fits into your broader kitchen maintenance.


What exactly does the dish washing service include?


We wash, rinse, and dry all dishes, glassware, pots, pans, and utensils in the sink or on the bench. Once everything is clean and dry, we put it away in the appropriate place. If there is a dishwasher, we can unload it and put items away as well.


Can I add dish washing to my existing cleaning booking?


Yes. It is one of the most common add-ons to our regular and [deep cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/deep-cleaning-melbourne/) bookings. Just let us know when you book or when you get in touch to adjust your service.


Is dish washing available as a standalone service?


It is available as an add-on to our Cleaning Services rather than as a standalone booking. It works best when combined with at least a standard clean so the rest of the kitchen is taken care of at the same time.


What if there is a large number of dishes?


We allow appropriate time based on what is described at booking. If the kitchen is particularly well used or there is a significant amount to wash, let us know in advance so we can factor it into the schedule.
