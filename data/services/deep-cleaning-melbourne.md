## Deep Cleaning FAQs

Find answers about timing, scope, preparation and when deep cleaning makes more sense than
[standard cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/standard-cleaning-melbourne/).


When should I book a deep clean versus a standard clean?


We recommend a deep clean if your home hasn't been professionally cleaned in the last 3
months, for seasonal refreshes (like spring cleaning), or when moving into a new property.
It targets hidden grime that [standard cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/standard-cleaning-melbourne/) doesn't reach.


Does Deep Cleaning include inside the oven
or fridge?


While Deep Cleaning is very intensive,
cleaning inside appliances like the oven or fridge
are considered optional add-ons. You can easily add these to your booking to ensure every
single part of your kitchen is spotless.


How long does a Deep Cleaning session take?


Deep Cleaning takes longer than standard
maintenance, typically between 4 and 8 hours
depending on the size and condition of the home. We focus on detail-heavy tasks like
scrubbing tile grout and cleaning skirting boards.


What results can I expect from a deep clean?


You can expect a home that feels truly "reset." We remove built-up grease in the kitchen,
limescale in the bathroom, and dust from often-neglected areas like door frames and light
fixtures, providing a hygiene level far above a standard wipe-down.
