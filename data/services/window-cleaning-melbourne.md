## Interior & Exterior Glass Cleaning Service

Windows are the primary source of natural light in any room, and the amount of light that actually comes through depends entirely on how clean the glass is. A film of dust, salt, and dried rain on the exterior surface reduces the light entering the room in ways that happen gradually and are rarely noticed until the windows are cleaned and the comparison is obvious. Clean windows make rooms feel larger, brighter, and significantly more comfortable to be in.

There is also the condition of the frames, sills, and tracks to consider. These areas collect insects, dirt, and moisture over time. Window tracks in particular develop buildup that makes the windows harder to operate and eventually finds its way into the room itself. Cleaning these properly is part of maintaining the window as a functioning feature of the home rather than just keeping the glass clear.

For tenants, windows are inspected closely at the end of a lease, and dirty tracks are one of the most consistently noted issues at final walkthroughs. Clean glass is expected as a baseline, but the condition of the frames and tracks is where the detail work shows. Getting a professional clean that covers all of it removes the risk of that being raised as a problem at the inspection.

## Window Cleaning FAQs

Answers about inclusions, access, frequency and how window cleaning fits into your broader property maintenance.


Does Window Cleaning include both the inside and outside of the glass?


We focus on professional interior window cleaning. We clean the interior glass, frames, sills, and tracks. We do not provide exterior-only window cleaning for high-rise or multi-story exteriors.


Can you clean windows on upper floors?


We can clean windows that are safely accessible from inside the property or from ground level outside. For windows on higher floors that require external equipment or ladder access, let us know when booking and we will confirm what is possible for your property.


How often should windows be professionally cleaned?


Most homes benefit from a proper window clean two to four times a year depending on the location and exposure. Properties near busy roads, in coastal areas, or with trees nearby tend to need more frequent cleaning. Inside glass can be included in regular cleans more frequently.


Will there be streaks left on the glass after cleaning?


No. We use professional grade Window Cleaning techniques and products that leave glass clear and streak free. If you notice any streaking after the clean, let us know and we will come back to address it.
