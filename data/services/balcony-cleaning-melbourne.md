## Outdoor Surface Rejuvenation Service

Your balcony is an extension of your living space, but it is also fully exposed to everything the outdoors brings. In Melbourne, that means UV damage, variable humidity, sea spray in coastal suburbs, and wind that deposits dust and debris all year round. Left unattended, even a relatively sheltered balcony will develop staining, slippery residue, and surface deterioration that becomes progressively harder to address.

There is also a safety dimension that is easy to overlook. Grime and organic material on balcony floors creates slip hazards, particularly after rain. Glass panels and railings that are not cleaned regularly develop a film that reduces visibility and can corrode or stain certain surfaces permanently over time. Keeping the balcony clean is part of keeping it safe and functional, not just presentable.

For tenants, the balcony is one of the most scrutinised areas at any final inspection. Property managers check outdoor surfaces carefully because deterioration and staining are difficult to reverse once they take hold. A clean balcony at handover removes a common source of bond disputes before they start.

Staying on top of balcony cleaning also protects the surface itself. Ground-in dirt is abrasive and accelerates wear on tiles and coatings. Regular cleaning keeps the surface in better condition for longer, which matters whether you own the property or are responsible for its upkeep as a tenant.

## Balcony Cleaning FAQs

Answers about surfaces, inclusions, timing and when balcony cleaning is worth adding to a broader booking.


What does balcony cleaning include?


We clean the floor surface including scrubbing out stains and built up dirt, the railings and barriers, any glass or perspex panels, and outdoor furniture if present. If there are specific areas you want us to focus on, just let us know when you book.


How often should a balcony be cleaned?


It depends on the location and exposure. Balconies in busier areas or higher floors that catch more wind and dust benefit from cleaning every couple of months. If you have birds visiting regularly, more frequent cleans make a real difference.


Is balcony cleaning included in a standard home clean?


Not by default. Our [standard cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/standard-cleaning-melbourne/) focuses on interior spaces. Balcony cleaning is available as an add-on and can be included in a deep clean or [end of lease](https://maidathome.com.au/services/end-of-lease-cleaning-melbourne/) clean on request.


Can you clean a balcony that has outdoor furniture on it?


Yes. We clean around and including outdoor furniture as part of the service. If there are items you would prefer we leave in place or move yourself, just let us know when booking and we will plan the visit accordingly.
