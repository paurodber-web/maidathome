## Deep Interior Sanitization Service

The fridge is the one appliance in the home that is in constant use and contains food at every stage of preparation. That makes the state of its interior a genuine food safety concern rather than a purely cosmetic one. Spills that are not cleaned promptly develop mould. Old food residue promotes bacterial growth. And the door seals, which are rarely checked, often develop the kind of mould that is invisible from a distance but very much present when looked at closely.

There is also a cross contamination risk that builds quietly over time. Raw and cooked foods stored in close proximity, on shelves that have not been properly cleaned, create conditions where bacteria from one source reaches another. Wiping shelves occasionally removes some of this, but a proper clean that removes the drawers and shelves and treats every surface is an entirely different standard of hygiene.

From an efficiency standpoint, buildup on shelves and around door seals forces the fridge to work harder to hold temperature. Over time this increases energy use and accelerates wear on the appliance itself. A clean fridge runs as designed, keeps food at the right temperature more consistently, and has a longer working life. The clean is an investment in the appliance, not just the appearance.

## Fridge Cleaning FAQs

Answers about timing, preparation, inclusions and how fridge cleaning fits into your broader property maintenance.


How long does Fridge Cleaning take?


A standard fridge typically takes between 30 and 45 minutes. If there is a lot of buildup or the fridge has not been cleaned in a long time, it may take a little longer. We always allow enough time to do it properly.


Do I need to empty the fridge before the cleaners arrive?


It helps if the fridge is mostly empty, but it is not essential. If you prefer, our team can remove items, place them safely aside while they clean, and put everything back in place once they are done.


Is Fridge Cleaning included in a standard home clean?


Our [standard cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/standard-cleaning-melbourne/) covers the exterior of the fridge as part of the kitchen. A dedicated fridge clean that covers the interior, shelves, drawers, and seals is available as an add-on. It is worth including if the inside needs proper attention.


Will the products used affect my food?


No. We use food-safe cleaning products that are effective at cutting through grease and odours without leaving residues that could contaminate food. Once the fridge is wiped dry, it is safe to use straight away.
