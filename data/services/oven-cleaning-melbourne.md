## Deep Carbon & Grease Removal Service

The oven is one of the hardest working appliances in any kitchen and one of the easiest to ignore until it starts
smoking, holding odours or looking impossible to clean. Grease and carbon residue do not just affect appearance. They
burn off during use, coat the interior again and make every future clean harder.

There are also practical safety and hygiene reasons to stay on top of it. Heavy grease buildup can increase smoke and
create a fire risk, while dirty racks, trays and glass immediately make the whole kitchen feel less cared for even after
the rest of the room has been cleaned.

For rentals, the oven is one of the first things checked at final inspection. Property managers open the door, inspect
the glass, trays, rails and visible grease lines. A professional oven clean before handover is one of the simplest ways
to avoid preventable issues during a move-out.

## Oven Cleaning FAQs

Answers about timing, inclusions, preparation and how oven cleaning fits into your broader property maintenance.


How long does a professional oven clean take?


Most standard ovens take between 45 minutes and one hour. If the oven has heavy buildup or has not been cleaned in a long time, it can take a little longer. We always allow enough time to do the job properly.


Is Oven Cleaning included in a standard clean?


Our [standard cleaning service](https://maidathome.com.au/services/standard-cleaning-melbourne/) covers the exterior of the oven as part of the kitchen. A dedicated oven clean that covers the interior, racks, trays, and door glass is available as an add-on. It is worth booking if your oven needs proper attention rather than a quick wipe.


Do I need to do anything before the cleaners arrive?


Just make sure the oven is cool and empty. There is no need to pre-clean or do anything else. Our team brings everything needed for the job.


Will the products you use leave a smell or be safe for cooking afterwards?


Yes, completely safe. We use professional cleaning solutions that are effective on grease without leaving harmful residues. Once the oven is wiped down and dry, it is ready to use straight away.
