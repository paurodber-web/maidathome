Many households start with a catch-up clean and quickly realise the bigger benefit is
not having to repeat the same recovery cycle every month. standard cleaning gives you
a dependable baseline, lowers how much grime compounds between visits and helps you
avoid turning every booking into a rescue mission.


It also makes specialist services more strategic. Instead of paying for deep-detail
labour every time, you can keep the house stable with standard visits and add targeted

What is included in a standard clean?


Our Standard Cleaning Service covers all the essentials: vacuuming and mopping floors,
dusting all accessible surfaces, sanitising kitchen countertops and bathrooms, and handling
light tidying such as emptying bins.


Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?


No, you don't need to provide anything. Our professional cleaners arrive fully equipped with
high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning supplies and commercial-grade equipment to ensure a
perfect result every time.


How long does a Standard Cleaning session take?


A typical Standard Cleaning session takes between 2 and 4 hours, depending on the size of
your home and its current condition. We work efficiently to provide a high-quality clean
without disrupting your daily routine.


Can I book recurring Cleaning Services?


Absolutely! Most of our clients prefer weekly or bi-weekly cleaning to keep their homes
consistently fresh. You can easily set up a recurring schedule during the booking process or
contact us to arrange one.
