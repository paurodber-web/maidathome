## Professional Spot & Scuff Removal

Walls are the largest surface in any room, but they are rarely included in a regular cleaning routine. Floors get mopped, benches get wiped, bathrooms get scrubbed, and walls get looked past until something makes them impossible to ignore. The result is that most homes carry a layer of marks, scuffs, and grime on their walls that accumulates slowly but changes how the space looks and feels in ways people often do not consciously connect.

The impact becomes most visible in high traffic areas. Hallways show scuffs and handprints at shoulder height. The wall beside a light switch collects fingerprints. The area above the stove carries grease and discolouration that builds from months of cooking. In family homes, it takes significantly less time for walls to show the full evidence of daily life. Crayon, pen, general marks, and the kind of gradual grime that no wipe can remove without the right products.

For rental properties, the condition of the walls is one of the most contentious aspects of any final inspection. Property managers look at walls room by room, noting every mark and area of deterioration. Marks that could have been cleaned are treated as tenant damage rather than fair wear and tear, which shifts the cost to the departing tenant. Getting the walls properly cleaned before the inspection removes the ambiguity and gives the property manager nothing to raise a dispute over.

## Wall Cleaning FAQs

Answers about inclusions, techniques and how wall cleaning fits into your broader property maintenance.


What types of marks can you remove from walls?


We treat fingerprints, scuffs, grease marks, general dirt buildup, and common household stains. Results depend on the type of mark, how long it has been there, and the paint or surface finish. Some deeper marks or stains may not come out completely, but we will always give you an honest assessment.


Will cleaning products damage my paint?


We use products appropriate for the wall finish and always test on a small area first where necessary. We take care to avoid anything that could strip, discolour, or damage the surface.


Is Wall Cleaning included in a standard home clean?

Can you clean all rooms or just specific areas?


We can clean all walls throughout the property or focus on specific rooms and high traffic areas depending on what you need. Just let us know when you book and we will plan the visit accordingly.
