## End of Lease Cleaning FAQs

Get answers about timing, guarantees, inclusions and when specialist services are worth adding
before inspection day.


Is my bond return collection guaranteed?


Yes, we follow strict agency-approved checklists used by Melbourne property managers. If
your manager identifies any cleaning-related issues during the final inspection, we'll
return to the property and rectify them free of charge within 72 hours.


Does it include professional carpet [carpet cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/carpet-cleaning-melbourne/)?


Professional carpet [carpet cleaning](https://maidathome.com.au/services/carpet-cleaning-melbourne/) is not included in the base package but is a highly
recommended add-on, as most rental agreements in Melbourne require it for bond return. You
can add it to your booking at a special discounted rate when bundled with our End of Lease
service.


What happens if the property manager isn't satisfied?


You simply send us the inspection report from your property manager. We prioritize these
cases and will coordinate a time to return to the property (usually within 24-48 hours) to
fix any items listed on the report at no extra cost to you.


Does the property need to be empty for the clean?


Ideally, yes. For the best result and to ensure we can clean inside every cupboard and
behind every appliance area, the property should be completely empty of furniture and
personal belongings before our team arrives.
