## Interior & Exterior Cabinet Detailing

Cabinet surfaces are in contact with food, cooking residue, and hands every single day. The grease that rises from cooking settles on fronts, handles, and edges in a thin, invisible film that builds up gradually and becomes harder to shift the longer it sits. Over months without a proper clean, that film becomes sticky, discoloured, and a genuine hygiene concern rather than just an aesthetic one.

The inside of cabinets often goes even longer without attention. Crumbs, old spills, and residue from stored packaging accumulate on shelves and create conditions that attract pests and bacteria. A kitchen that looks clean on the surface may have interior cupboards that have not been properly cleaned in years. That matters in any home, but it is especially important in a kitchen where food is being prepared and stored.

For rental properties, cabinets are checked meticulously at final inspections. Property managers open every door and examine every shelf. Clean cabinet exteriors and interiors signal a well maintained property and remove one of the most common grounds for bond deductions. Getting them professionally cleaned before handover is a straightforward step that makes a significant difference to the outcome.

## Cabinet Cleaning FAQs

Answers about inclusions, preparation, surfacing and how cabinet cleaning fits into your broader kitchen maintenance.


Does cabinet cleaning include the inside of the cabinets?


Yes. We clean both the exterior surfaces, including doors, handles, and edges, and the interior shelves. If you would prefer we focus only on the outside, just let us know and we will adjust the service.


Do I need to empty the cabinets before the cleaners arrive?


It helps to have the cabinets reasonably clear, particularly the ones you want cleaned inside. Our team can work around dishes and items if needed, but an empty shelf will always get a more thorough clean.


Will the cleaning products damage my cabinet finish?


We use products suited to the cabinet material and finish. We avoid anything abrasive on painted, laminate, or timber surfaces. If your cabinets have a specific finish you are concerned about, let us know when booking and we will come prepared.


How often should kitchen cabinets be properly cleaned?


It depends on how much cooking happens in the home. A busy kitchen benefits from a proper cabinet clean every three to four months. Less frequently used kitchens can go longer between thorough cleans.
