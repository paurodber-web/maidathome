### Removes 99% of Bacteria & Allergens From Carpet Fibres

Standard vacuuming removes surface debris but leaves bacteria and
dust mites embedded in the fibres. Steam penetrates where vacuums can't reach, which is why it's
the method recommended for households with allergy sufferers and young children.



02.

### Non-Toxic, Pet-Safe Steam Cleaning Products

Many industrial cleaning products leave residue that pets and
toddlers come into direct contact with on the floor. Every product we use is biodegradable and free
from ammonia, bleach and synthetic fragrances, and is safe the moment it dries.



03.
Lasting Care

### Extends Carpet Life, Professional Restoration Methods

Dirt and grit trapped in carpet fibres act like sandpaper, breaking
down the material from the inside. Regular professional steam cleaning slows that wear, and most carpets last significantly longer with annual professional maintenance than with vacuuming alone.
