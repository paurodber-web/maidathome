### Removes 99% of Bacteria & Allergens From Carpet Fibres

Standard vacuuming removes surface debris but leaves bacteria and
dust mites embedded in the fibres. Steam penetrates where vacuums can't reach, which is why it's
the method recommended for households with allergy sufferers and young children.

### Non-Toxic, Pet-Safe Steam Cleaning Products

Many industrial cleaning products leave residue that pets and

03.
Lasting Care

### Extends Carpet Life, Professional Restoration Methods
