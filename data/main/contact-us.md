## Some  Common Questions

Find everything you need to know about our Cleaning Services, booking process, and how we handle
your home.


What makes Maid At Home different?


At Maid At Home, we're all about quality, reliability, and a personal touch. Over 90% of our clients
return because we genuinely care about making their homes feel fresh and comfortable. We focus on
attention to detail, transparent pricing, and flexible options to suit your needs. If you want to compare
the options first, you can explore our [cleaning services](https://maidathome.com.au/services/) and review our [pricing](https://maidathome.com.au/pricing/).


Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?


Yes! We take pride in our work and stand by our service. If you're not completely satisfied, let us know
within 24 hours, and we'll make it right.


Are there any lock-in contracts?


No, there are no lock-in contracts! We believe in earning your trust through quality cleaning, not
contracts. When you're ready, you can [book online](https://maidathome.com.au/booking/) and we will confirm the next step with you.


How are your cleaners vetted?


We carefully select and screen all our cleaners through a thorough vetting process, which includes
background checks, interviews, and skills assessments. We also ensure they align with our high standards
of professionalism and customer care.


Are your cleaning products eco-friendly?


Yes, we use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the
environment. Our goal is to provide a clean home while minimizing our impact on the planet.
