Maid At Home provides residential cleaning services across selected Melbourne suburbs. This policy applies when you use our website, make a booking, contact us, receive a service or otherwise interact with us.

We aim to handle personal information transparently and in accordance with applicable Australian privacy law, including the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles where they apply.

01 / SCOPE

About this policy.

In this policy, “Maid At Home”, “we”, “us” and “our” refer to the business operating under the Maid At Home name. “You” means a customer, prospective customer, website visitor or other person whose information we handle.

Personal information is information or an opinion about an identified individual, or an individual who is reasonably identifiable. Some information may not identify you on its own but may do so when combined with other information.

Our practical approach

We collect only the information reasonably needed for a genuine business purpose and limit access to people who need it to perform their role.

02 / COLLECTION

Information we may collect.

The information we collect depends on how you interact with us and the service you request. It may include:

  • your name, email address, telephone number and billing details
  • your service address, suburb, property type, bedrooms, bathrooms and parking details
  • booking dates, service type, frequency, extras, price and service history
  • access instructions, lockbox or concierge details and information about pets
  • service preferences, priority notes, feedback, complaints and communications
  • photos you choose to send us, including images of the property or areas requiring attention
  • payment status, transaction references and limited billing information provided by our payment processor
  • website information such as IP address, browser type, device details, pages visited and cookie data.

Sensitive or unnecessary information

Please do not provide sensitive information unless it is reasonably necessary for us to safely deliver the service. If you send us information we did not request, we will decide whether it is reasonably necessary to retain it and may delete or de-identify it where appropriate.

03 / SOURCES

How we collect information.

We usually collect personal information directly from you when you book online, contact us, send a message or photo, provide access instructions, complete a payment, leave feedback or speak with our team.

We may also receive information from:

  • a person making a booking on your behalf
  • our booking, payment, communication and website service providers
  • a cleaner or service provider who needs to report an access, safety or service issue
  • publicly available sources where reasonably necessary.

Where practical, you may interact with us anonymously or using a pseudonym. However, we generally need your identity, address and contact details to provide an in-home cleaning service.

04 / USE

How we use personal information.

We may use personal information to:

  • provide quotes, accept and manage bookings and confirm service availability
  • match a suitable cleaner or team and share the information needed to attend the property
  • process payments, authorisations, refunds and approved adjustments
  • communicate about assignments, reminders, changes, access and service follow-up
  • respond to enquiries, complaints, damage reports and requests for a re-clean
  • maintain customer records and recurring service preferences
  • improve our website, booking process, services, training and customer support
  • prevent fraud, protect our systems, manage safety and enforce our terms
  • meet legal, accounting, insurance and regulatory obligations.

Direct marketing

We may send service updates or marketing communications where permitted and where you have not opted out. You can unsubscribe using the link in the message or contact us at any time. Operational messages about an active booking are not marketing messages.

05 / DISCLOSURE

When we share information.

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose it where reasonably necessary to:

  • cleaners, teams and other service providers organised to perform your booking
  • booking, payment, hosting, email, messaging, analytics and customer support providers
  • professional advisers such as accountants, lawyers and insurers
  • law enforcement, regulators, courts or government bodies where required or authorised by law
  • a purchaser or adviser in connection with a proposed sale or restructure of the business, subject to appropriate confidentiality arrangements.

We limit the information shared with cleaners and providers to what they reasonably need. For example, a cleaner may need the service address, access instructions, booking scope and a contact method, but does not need your full payment card details.

Overseas processing

Some technology providers may store or process information outside Australia. The countries involved can change as providers update their infrastructure. Where applicable, we take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and protect information in accordance with Australian privacy requirements.

06 / PAYMENTS

Payment information.

Payments and card authorisations are processed through third-party payment and booking providers. Maid At Home does not intentionally store your complete card number or card security code in its ordinary business systems.

We may receive and retain limited payment information such as the card type, last digits, transaction reference, authorisation status, payment amount and refund status. Your use of the payment provider may also be subject to that provider's privacy policy and terms.

07 / WEBSITE

Website data and cookies.

Our website and providers may use cookies and similar technologies to make the website function, remember preferences, understand usage, measure performance and help protect the booking process.

Essential technologiesSupport security, navigation, booking and other functions requested by you.
Analytics technologiesHelp us understand aggregated website activity and improve the experience.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect website or booking functionality. Third-party links and embedded tools are governed by the privacy practices of their respective operators.

08 / PROTECTION

Security and retention.

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. These measures may include access controls, reputable service providers, account security practices and limiting information to people who need it.

No system is completely secure. If you believe information connected with Maid At Home has been compromised, contact us promptly.

How long we keep information

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including service history, customer support, accounting, taxation, insurance, dispute resolution and legal requirements. When information is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it, subject to backups and lawful retention obligations.

09 / YOUR CHOICES

Accessing and correcting your information.

You may request access to personal information we hold about you or ask us to correct information that is inaccurate, out of date, incomplete, irrelevant or misleading.

Send your request to hello@maidathome.com.au. We may need to verify your identity before responding. In some circumstances, applicable law permits or requires us to refuse access. If that happens, we will explain the reason where we are permitted to do so.

Please tell us when your contact, service or access details change so we can keep active booking information accurate.

10 / CONCERNS

Privacy questions and complaints.

If you have a privacy concern, email us with the subject line “Privacy”. Include enough information for us to understand the issue and identify the relevant record or interaction.

We will acknowledge and investigate complaints within a reasonable period. If you are not satisfied with our response and applicable privacy law gives you the right to do so, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our services, systems or legal obligations change. The current version will be published on our website with an updated effective date. Material changes may also be communicated directly where appropriate.

11 / CONTACT

Contact Maid At Home.

For access, correction, privacy questions or complaints, contact our team and include “Privacy” in the subject line.
