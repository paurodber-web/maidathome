Maid At Home respects your privacy. We handle personal information in line with applicable Australian privacy law, including the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles where they apply.

1. Scope of this policy

This policy applies when you visit our website, request a quote, make or manage a booking, contact our team, provide feedback, apply to work with us or otherwise use our residential cleaning services.

“Maid At Home”, “we”, “us” and “our” refer to the business operating the Maid At Home residential cleaning service in Melbourne, Victoria.

2. Information we collect

The information we collect depends on how you interact with us and may include:

  • your name, email address, telephone number and billing details;
  • your service address, property type, bedrooms, bathrooms and booking preferences;
  • access, key or lockbox instructions, parking information and whether you will be home;
  • information about pets, allergies, product preferences, priorities and other details needed to deliver the service safely;
  • booking history, messages, complaints, photographs you choose to send and service feedback;
  • payment status and limited transaction details supplied by our payment provider;
  • website usage data, device information, IP address and cookie identifiers; and
  • identity, experience, availability, insurance and work-related information from cleaners or applicants.

We generally collect information directly from you. We may also receive it from another person making a booking for you, a property manager, our service providers or a cleaner assigned to your booking.

3. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • provide estimates, arrange bookings and match your service with an available cleaner or team;
  • communicate booking confirmations, arrival information, changes and service updates;
  • process payments, holds, refunds and invoices;
  • manage safety, access, parking, quality concerns, re-cleans, damage reports and disputes;
  • respond to enquiries and improve our services, website and customer experience;
  • prevent fraud, protect our legal rights and comply with legal obligations; and
  • send marketing communications where you have consented or where permitted by law.

If you do not provide information required for a booking, we may be unable to arrange or safely complete the service.

4. Who we share information with

We disclose only what is reasonably necessary to operate the service. Recipients may include:

  • independent cleaners and cleaning teams assigned to your booking;
  • booking, email, hosting, analytics, customer support and payment service providers;
  • professional advisers, insurers, government bodies or law enforcement where required; and
  • a purchaser or adviser involved in a genuine business restructure or sale.

Cleaners receive the booking details they need to locate the property and complete the agreed service. They are not permitted to use those details for unrelated purposes.

Overseas processing

Some technology providers may process or store information outside Australia. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and appropriate safeguards, while recognising that overseas privacy laws may differ.

5. Payments and card information

Online card payments are processed by a third-party payment provider such as Stripe. Maid At Home does not receive or store your complete card number or security code. We may receive a payment token, card type, last four digits, expiry information and transaction status so that we can manage your booking.

The payment provider handles your card information under its own privacy policy and security standards.

6. Marketing, analytics and cookies

Our website may use essential cookies and limited analytics technologies to keep the site working, understand page performance and improve the booking experience. You can restrict cookies through your browser, although some functions may then work differently.

We may contact you about relevant services or offers where permitted. You can unsubscribe using the link in a marketing email or by contacting us. Service messages about an active booking are not marketing and may still be sent.

7. Security and retention

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

We retain information only for as long as reasonably needed for service delivery, record keeping, taxation, insurance, dispute management and legal compliance. Information that is no longer required is deleted or de-identified where practical.

8. Accessing or correcting your information

You may ask to access the personal information we hold about you or request a correction if it is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date. Contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

In limited circumstances we may lawfully refuse access. If so, we will explain the reason where permitted.

9. Contact, complaints and policy changes

For privacy questions, access or correction requests, or a complaint, email hello@maidathome.com.au. Please provide enough detail for us to investigate. We will acknowledge and respond within a reasonable period.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner through oaic.gov.au.

We may update this policy when our practices, technology or legal obligations change. The current version and update date will remain available on this page.
