Please read these terms before booking. By confirming a booking, you agree to these terms and confirm that you are authorised to arrange access to the property.

1. Agreement and eligibility

These Terms and Conditions form an agreement between you and Maid At Home for the arrangement of residential cleaning services in Melbourne, Victoria. They apply to bookings made online, by email, telephone or any other agreed method.

You must be at least 18 years old and provide accurate contact, property, access and payment information. If you book for another person or property, you confirm that you have authority to do so and will ensure the occupant understands these terms.

2. Our service model

Maid At Home operates as a booking and referral service. Cleaning services may be performed by independent cleaners or cleaning teams who are responsible for delivering the work assigned to them. We coordinate bookings, communication and payments and require service providers to maintain appropriate insurance and professional standards.

We aim to provide the same cleaner for recurring bookings where practical, but cannot guarantee a particular person. We may arrange a replacement when a cleaner is unavailable.

3. Bookings, estimates and pricing

A booking is confirmed when we accept it and send confirmation. An automated request or payment authorisation alone does not guarantee availability. Arrival times may vary by approximately 15–30 minutes because of traffic, parking or the previous service.

Prices shown online are in Australian dollars and may be estimates or starting prices. Final pricing depends on the selected service, property size, condition, access, extras and information provided. You must tell us before the service about unusually heavy build-up, renovations, hazards or requirements not included in the booking.

Hourly services

Hourly bookings cover the cleaner’s time, not a guaranteed number of rooms or tasks. Priorities are completed in the order agreed, subject to the available time. A minimum booking duration may apply. Where multiple cleaners attend, booked labour time is divided between them.

Flat-rate services

Flat rates apply to the described scope for the property details supplied. Additional time or charges may be proposed if those details are inaccurate, the condition is materially different or extra work is requested. We will seek approval before undertaking chargeable work outside the booking where reasonably possible.

4. Payment authorisation and charges

You must provide a valid payment method unless another arrangement is confirmed in writing. By booking, you authorise our payment provider to store a payment token and Maid At Home to place a temporary authorisation hold, charge the final amount, process approved extras and issue refunds or adjustments connected with the booking.

A hold may be placed up to two days before the service, or immediately for short-notice bookings. The final charge is generally processed after service completion. Your bank controls how quickly unused holds are released.

If payment fails, we may retry, request another method, suspend future bookings or recover reasonable collection costs. Direct payment to a cleaner is permitted only when Maid At Home has expressly confirmed that arrangement.

5. Rescheduling and cancellations

Please tell us as early as possible if plans change.

  • A booking may be rescheduled without a fee when at least 24 hours’ notice is provided and the replacement service is completed within seven days, subject to availability. This waiver normally applies once per booking.
  • A cancellation or change made less than 24 hours before the scheduled start may incur a fee of 50% of the booking value.
  • If the cleaner has arrived, is on the way, cannot enter, or the service cannot proceed because of unsafe conditions or unavailable parking/access, up to 100% of the booking value may be charged.

Recurring discounts apply from the second completed service. If a recurring schedule is cancelled before the second service, the first visit may be repriced at the applicable one-off rate.

We may reschedule or cancel because of cleaner illness, safety concerns, severe weather, operational disruption or circumstances beyond reasonable control. We will offer a replacement where practical or refund any amount paid for work not supplied.

6. Property access, parking and safety

You must provide safe, timely access and accurate entry instructions. Maid At Home does not routinely hold customer keys. A lockbox, concierge or secure agreed location should be used where you will not be present. Do not label a key with a full address.

You must provide lawful parking reasonably close to the property and disclose any fees, permits, height limits or restrictions. Parking costs may be added to the booking. If suitable parking cannot be found after a reasonable attempt, the service may be treated as a late cancellation.

Please secure valuables, fragile or sentimental items, confidential documents and unstable furniture. Tell us about alarms, pets, aggressive animals, allergies, hazards, broken fixtures, delicate surfaces and any area that must not be entered. Cleaners do not handle animal or human waste, pest infestations, needles, illegal substances or other biohazards.

For health and cross-contamination reasons, customers may be asked to provide a working vacuum cleaner and mop. If you cannot, tell us before booking so we can try to arrange a suitably equipped cleaner.

7. What is and is not included

The included work is determined by the service description and extras shown at booking. Standard cleaning is a maintenance service; deep, move-in and end-of-lease services have different scopes. End-of-lease cleaning is designed for an empty property and includes the inside of the oven and cabinets only where stated.

Unless expressly agreed, services exclude:

  • moving heavy furniture or appliances, high or unsafe access, ceilings and exterior work;
  • deep grout restoration, mould remediation, pest treatment, pressure washing and garden or pool work;
  • removal of paint, adhesives, permanent staining, silicone discolouration or damage requiring specialist restoration;
  • cleaning between oven glass, air-conditioning filters, curtains, flyscreens, garages, barbecues and external windows; and
  • waste removal, hazardous materials, bodily fluids or animal excrement.

Results depend on age, wear, previous damage and material condition. Cleaning cannot restore a surface beyond its original condition or guarantee removal of permanent stains.

8. Quality concerns and re-clean support

If you believe an included task was missed or not completed to a reasonable standard, contact us promptly with clear details and photographs. General cleaning concerns should usually be reported within 48 hours. End-of-lease concerns must be reported within seven days.

Where the concern is valid, our usual remedy is to coordinate a reasonable re-clean of the affected areas. You must provide access within a reasonable time. A refund is not automatically available where a re-clean has been offered and refused, where another provider has altered the area, or where the issue falls outside the booked scope.

Hourly quality support concerns the reasonable quality of completed tasks; it does not guarantee that every requested task will fit within the booked time. Extended guarantees may not apply to carpet, upholstery or steam services.
