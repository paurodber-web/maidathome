Where it started

We began on the other side of the booking.

Before Maid At Home had a name, we worked in cleaning and learned the job from the inside: the physical effort, the small details that change a room and the trust involved when someone opens their home to you.

In 2017, we began cleaning homes ourselves. We wanted to create the kind of service we believed people deserved: friendly without being casual, professional without feeling distant and organised without making things complicated.

“The standard was simple: treat every home with the same care we would want in our own.”

That hands-on beginning still shapes every decision we make, from how we communicate with customers to how we support the people completing each clean.

How our team works, what guides us and what you can expect when you welcome us into your home.

Ask us anything

Yes. Maid At Home began in Melbourne in 2017 and is built around the homes, neighbourhoods and routines of the people who live here.

Pau and Stefania lead the business with hands-on cleaning experience and a shared belief that friendly service and reliable systems should always go together. Our cat provides encouragement of a less practical kind.

We look for people who combine practical cleaning ability with reliability, care and respectful communication. Every home involves trust, so how someone works matters just as much as the final result.

We aim to keep the same cleaner and familiar routine wherever availability allows. If a change is required, we organise an experienced replacement and keep you informed.

If something appears to have been missed, contact us within 24 hours. We review the details promptly and coordinate the appropriate next step rather than leaving you to chase an answer.

Because a cleaning service affects more than a room. When the booking is simple, communication is clear and the home feels cared for, customers get back a little time and calm as well.
