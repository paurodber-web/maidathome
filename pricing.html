Pricing questions

Everything worth knowing before you book.

If your home has unusual requirements, include them in the booking notes so we can review them early.

Are the displayed flat rates final?
They are starting prices based primarily on bedrooms. Bathrooms, condition, property details and selected extras may affect the final estimate.
When do recurring discounts begin?
Recurring discounts begin from the second completed service. Weekly and fortnightly bookings receive 10% off; three-weekly and monthly bookings receive 5% off.
Is there a minimum hourly booking?
Yes. Hourly cleaning has a two-hour minimum. If two cleaners attend for one hour, that equals two booked cleaning hours.
Are cleaning products included?
Cleaning products are included. Clients generally provide a mop and vacuum to reduce cross-contamination between homes. If needed, ask us whether an equipped team is available.
What happens if more time is needed?
For hourly services, the team follows your priority list within the booked time. If additional time appears necessary, we seek approval before continuing whenever possible.
When will my card be charged?
Your card is securely stored when booking. A temporary hold is generally placed two days before the service, and the payment is captured after the clean.
