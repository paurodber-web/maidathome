What shapes the price

Four choices shape the price.

The calculator changes only when one of these practical parts of the booking changes.

01

Service type

Standard, Deep, Move In, End of Lease and Hourly each have a different scope and starting rate.

02

Bedrooms or hours

Flat rates scale with the number of bedrooms. Hourly cleaning scales with booked team time.

03

Frequency

Eligible recurring bookings receive 10% or 5% off from their second service.

04

Extras

Optional detail tasks are added separately, including quantities where relevant.
