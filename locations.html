How local availability works

Suburb, date and the right team all matter.

When you book

Tell us the exact address.

Your suburb confirms the general service area; the address helps us review travel, access and parking.

Before confirmation

We match nearby availability.

We coordinate a suitable cleaner and confirm the booking by email once the assignment is complete.

Location questions

Useful details about our service area.

Coverage and team availability can vary by date, especially near the outer edge of our usual radius.

Do you clean homes outside the listed suburbs?
Sometimes a nearby address may still be possible depending on the date and team location. Contact us with the full address so we can check before you book.
Do you service all of Greater Melbourne?
Our core service area is approximately 15 kilometres from Melbourne CBD. We do not currently service distant regional locations such as Geelong.
Does being listed guarantee availability?
The list confirms general coverage, but a specific date still depends on team schedules, travel time and the service requested.
Is parking required?
Clients need to provide suitable parking or clear parking guidance. Reliable nearby access is important, particularly for teams carrying equipment or providing steam cleaning.
Can I book recurring cleaning in my suburb?
Recurring cleaning is available throughout the core service area, subject to finding a suitable ongoing time and cleaner for your location.
