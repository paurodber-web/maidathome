Can I change my booking without a fee?
Rescheduling is free when requested at least 24 hours beforehand and the service is completed within seven days of the original date. A booking can only be postponed once.
How do I report a quality concern?
Email clear details and photos as soon as possible. General concerns should be reported within 48 hours; End of Lease re-clean requests have a seven-day contact window.
Can I request a team that brings a mop and vacuum?
Yes. Add this to your booking or enquiry. Availability can be more limited because clients generally provide their own equipment to reduce cross-contamination.
Can I ask for the same cleaner?
Yes. We try to keep the same cleaner for recurring bookings whenever possible, although availability, illness or personal circumstances may occasionally require a change.
