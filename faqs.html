Booking and arrival

How do I book and when is it confirmed?
Complete the online form with your service, property details, preferred date and any extras. We receive the request and work on assigning a suitable team. The cleaner assignment is confirmed by email once organised.
How much notice should I give?
More notice gives us a better chance of matching your preferred date and time. Very short-notice bookings may not be possible, particularly when submitted outside opening hours.
What is the arrival window?
Allow a 15–30 minute arrival window after the booked time for traffic and delays between homes. If the team expects a longer delay, we will communicate the available options.
What happens if the cleaner is more than 30 minutes late?
You may choose to wait longer, reschedule or cancel. Contact us so we can confirm the delay and coordinate the option that works for you.
Can I book on weekends or public holidays?
Saturday bookings are available. Sundays and public holidays depend on whether a cleaner is available and willing to work. Maid At Home does not add a weekend or public-holiday surcharge.
02

Services and timing

What is the difference between Standard and Deep cleaning?
Standard Clean maintains the main areas and everyday surfaces of a generally cared-for home. Deep / Spring Clean gives more attention to built-up dust, detail work and areas outside routine maintenance.
What is included in an End of Lease Clean?
End of Lease is designed for an empty property and includes a comprehensive clean of the booked scope, the inside of cabinets and one oven. Additional ovens are charged separately.
How does Hourly Cleaning work?
You book a minimum of two hours and give the cleaner a priority list. Tasks are completed in that order as time allows. The service guarantees the quality of completed work rather than a fixed number of rooms.
How do hours work when two cleaners attend?
The booked time is divided by team size. For example, two cleaners working for one hour deliver two total cleaning hours.
Is steam cleaning always available?
Carpet and upholstery steam cleaning depends on specialist availability. If no provider is available, we will contact you to change or remove that part of the booking without a cancellation fee.
03

Pricing and payment

What are the hourly rates?
One-off Hourly Cleaning is $60 per hour. Weekly and fortnightly bookings are $54 per hour, while three-weekly and monthly bookings are $57 per hour. Recurring discounts begin from the second service.
What is the minimum booking?
Hourly cleaning has a two-hour minimum. Flat-rate services are priced by the defined service scope and property details rather than a minimum number of hours.
When is my card held and charged?
Your card is entered securely through the booking form. A temporary authorisation hold is generally placed two days before the service. If the booking is made within two days, the hold may be placed immediately. The card is charged after completion.
Do you accept cash or bank transfer?
New online bookings are paid by credit or debit card through Stripe. Alternative payment arrangements are only available in limited cases when approved directly by administration.
04

Changes and cancellations

Can I reschedule without a fee?
There is no rescheduling fee when the request is made at least 24 hours beforehand and the service is completed within seven days of the original date. A booking may only be postponed once.
What is the cancellation fee?
Cancellation with less than 24 hours' notice is charged at 50% of the booking. If the team is already on the way or arrives and the service cannot proceed, the fee is 100%.
What if nobody can provide access?
The cleaner will contact you and wait up to approximately 15 minutes. If access still cannot be provided, the service may be treated as a same-day cancellation.
Can a recurring booking be paused indefinitely?
No. A service can be postponed once and must be completed within seven days to avoid the applicable cancellation fee. Longer changes should be discussed with the team.
05

Your home, access and pets

Do I need to provide a mop and vacuum?
Yes, in most cases. Using the household's own mop and vacuum helps reduce cross-contamination between homes. If you do not have them, request an equipped team during booking; availability may be limited.
Are cleaning products included?
Yes. Standard cleaning products are included. If your home has delicate surfaces, allergies or product restrictions, add clear notes before the service.
Can the cleaner keep my keys?
No. We recommend a lockbox, concierge or another secure access arrangement. If a key is left, label it without including a full name or address.
Are pets okay during the clean?
Pets are welcome, but they must not interfere with the cleaner. Tell us about pets, allergies or fears when booking. Pet waste, litter boxes, cages and similar tasks are excluded.
Do I need to provide parking?
Yes. Provide suitable nearby parking or clear instructions. If parking cannot be found after a reasonable attempt, the service may be unable to proceed. Steam-cleaning parking must be within 50 metres.
Do I need to be home?
No. Many clients provide secure access and leave clear booking notes. You may also remain home if preferred, provided the cleaner can safely access and work through the booked areas.
06

Cleaners and satisfaction

Can I have the same cleaner every time?
We aim to keep the same cleaner for recurring clients whenever possible. A change may occasionally be necessary because of availability, illness, holidays or personal circumstances.
Are cleaners insured?
Cleaners working through Maid At Home are independent professionals and are expected to maintain appropriate insurance and experience for residential work.
What should I do if something was missed?
Contact us promptly with clear details and photos. General quality concerns should be reported within 48 hours. We review the booked scope and, where appropriate, coordinate a re-clean before considering another resolution.
How does the End of Lease re-clean support work?
Contact us within seven days with the property manager's feedback and supporting photos. Re-clean support applies to tasks included in the original booked scope and is coordinated subject to access and weekday availability.
